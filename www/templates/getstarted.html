How to install Notify.io your Mac

1. Download Nio, the Notify.io Mac client

Click here to start the download.

2. Drag the Nio icon into your Applications folder.

3. Double click the Nio icon in your Applications folder.

Nio is now running, but needs to be configured to receive your notifications.

4. Download and open the Desktop Notifier configuration for your account.

Click here to download. When asked, choose to Open the file. Make sure to open the file with Nio.app

5. You're now ready to receive notifications from any Notify.io source!

From here you can:

  • Look for sites you use that support Notify.io from our available sources.
  • Add alternative notifiers such as email, Jabber, or Twitter.
  • Learn how you can add notifications to your site on our Developers page.
